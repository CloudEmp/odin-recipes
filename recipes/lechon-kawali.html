Lechon Kawali

About this Dish

A crispy Filipino fried pork dish

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork belly
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 2 cups of oil

Steps

  1. Combine pork belly, garlic, bay leaves, salt, and pepper in a skillet; pour in enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until skin is tender, 35 to 45 minutes.
  2. Drain and let sit at room temperature until pork has air-dried.
  3. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. Fry pork in the hot oil, working in batches, until golden brown, 3 to 6 minutes.
  5. Remove fried pork with a slotted spoon and drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
